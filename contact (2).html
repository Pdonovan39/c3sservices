Common questions
What people ask before they reach out
How quickly can you get a team started?
For staff augmentation, we can typically have engineers embedded within days. For a scoped engagement where we're hiring specifically for your project, the timeline depends on the role and stack — but we move fast. We'll give you a realistic timeline on our first call.
Do I have to commit to a long contract?
No. Our engagements can be terminated with just a few weeks of notice. We don't lock clients into long contracts because we don't need to — we retain clients through the quality of our work, not contract terms.
What does the Project Manager cost?
Nothing extra. Every C3S client gets a dedicated Project Manager included at no additional charge. They're your single point of contact for day-to-day communication, sprint updates, and issue escalation.
How do you handle communication across time zones?
Our engineering team in Cairo operates with US-timezone alignment. Your dedicated PM ensures you always have a responsive point of contact. Clients in the US have consistently found communication to be one of our strongest differentiators.
What if I don't know exactly what I need yet?
That's fine — most of our best engagements started with a general problem, not a detailed spec. Book a call, tell us what you're trying to accomplish, and we'll help you figure out the right approach and team structure.
Can C3S work alongside our existing team?
Absolutely. Most of our engagements involve embedding engineers directly into an existing client team — working in your Jira, your Slack, your standups. We integrate into your process, not the other way around.
