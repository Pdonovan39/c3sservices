Our approach
The hardest part of enterprise AI isn't the model.

It's the integration. The context. The trust. That's exactly where we focus — embedding our engineers inside your organization so they understand your data, your workflows, and your constraints before writing a single line of AI code.

"Forward-deployed AI engineering means we're not building AI for you from a distance. We're building it with you, from the inside."
Week 1–2
Discovery & context
We embed with your team, map your workflows, and identify the highest-value AI use cases — no generic recommendations.
Week 3–6
Build & integrate
We build the agents, train on your data, and integrate into your existing stack — Agile, iterative, and fully transparent.
Month 2–3
Test & refine
Real-world testing with your team, rapid iteration, and quality gates before anything goes to production.
Month 4–6
Production & scale
Agents live in production. You own the code, the models, and the infrastructure — built to scale without us.
