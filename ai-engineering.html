Our approach
We embed, discover, build, and deliver.

Every AI engagement is different. We start by understanding your workflows, data, and goals before recommending anything. Then we build iteratively — in close collaboration with your team — until agents are running reliably in your environment.

Phase 1
Discovery & context

We embed with your team, map your workflows, and identify the highest-value AI use cases before recommending anything. The right starting point varies by organization.

Phase 2
Build & integrate

We build iteratively — connecting your data sources, integrating into your existing stack, and keeping your team involved at every step.

Phase 3
Test & refine

Real-world testing with your team and stakeholders. We iterate until performance meets the bar — quality is non-negotiable before anything goes to production.

Phase 4
Deliver & hand over

You own everything we build. Full code handover, documentation, and knowledge transfer so your team can run and extend it independently.
